49. Group Anagrams   
Given an array of strings, group anagrams together.

For example, given: ["eat", "tea", "tan", "ate", "nat", "bat"], 
Return:

[
  ["ate", "eat","tea"],
  ["nat","tan"],
  ["bat"]
]
Note: All inputs will be in lower-case.


Key point:
sort all the words as their key
insert them into the multiple map, extract the result;
